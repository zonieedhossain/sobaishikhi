# SobaiShikhi (সবাইশিখি)

### "যে কেউ শেখাবে, সবাই শিখবে" — Anyone can teach, everyone can learn

Bangladesh's all-in-one Bangla **knowledge, repair, commerce & service ecosystem** — combining the best of **Udemy + iFixit + JustAnswer + Daraz + Fiverr** in one trust-first platform.

---

## 🚀 The prototype

**[`index.html`](index.html)** — a working high-fidelity prototype. Use the **role switcher** (bottom of screen) to explore all sides:

- **Browse** — public marketplace: home, search, categories, course detail, repair hub, store, experts, blog/forum.
- **Learn** — learner dashboard: continue learning, progress, certificates, wishlist.
- **Teach** — instructor panel: course builder, submit-for-review, earnings.
- **Vendor / Expert** — sell tools & parts; offer video consultations & write repair guides.
- **Admin** — the control center: Review Center, Orders, users, categories, payouts, platform settings.

> Everything public passes through **admin review** before going live. Bangla content, English UI chrome, local payments (bKash/Nagad/Rocket).

## 🎯 The one-liner

> A knowledge economy platform where Bangladeshis **learn skills, fix problems, and earn from expertise** — all in Bangla, all in one trusted place.

## 👥 Who it's for

Learners · Instructors · Repair experts · Vendors · Technicians · Buyers — and an **Admin** team that reviews and controls everything.

## 💰 How it makes money

Commission across **courses (~20%), tools & parts (~10%), consultations (~15%), services (~12%)**, plus subscriptions, certifications, and advertising — many revenue surfaces on one acquired user.

## 📈 Growth strategy

**Curated → instructor expansion → open marketplace.** Seed quality supply, use the free Repair Hub as a near-zero-CAC traffic magnet, then scale demand once economics prove out.
