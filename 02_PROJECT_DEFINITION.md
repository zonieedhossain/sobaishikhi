# 02 — Project Definition

## Project Name

**SobaiShikhi (সবাইশিখি)** — "যে কেউ শেখাবে, সবাই শিখবে" (_Anyone can teach, everyone can learn_)

A Bangla-first **knowledge, repair, commerce & service ecosystem** for Bangladesh — combining the best of Udemy, iFixit, JustAnswer, Daraz and Fiverr inside one trusted platform.

---

## What problem am I solving?

Quality, practical education and reliable repair help in Bangladesh are **fragmented, English-dominated, and hard to trust.**

- Learners can't find affordable, **Bangla** courses for real, job-ready skills.
- Experts (teachers, technicians, mechanics, scholars) have valuable knowledge but **no easy way to package it, reach an audience, or earn from it.**
- When a device or appliance breaks, people have **nowhere trustworthy** to learn the fix, ask an expert, buy the right tool/part, or book a verified technician — they rely on scattered Facebook groups and word of mouth.
- Existing platforms create their own content (slow, narrow). Nobody has built the **marketplace + repair economy** layer for Bangladesh.

---

## Who are my users?

1. **Learners** — students, job-seekers, freelancers, homemakers, hobbyists who want affordable skills in Bangla.
2. **Instructors** — teachers, professionals, religious scholars, creators who want to teach and earn.
3. **Repair experts & contributors** — technicians, mechanics, engineers who write repair guides, answer questions, and take video consultations.
4. **Vendors & sellers** — tool shops, parts distributors, independent sellers listing on the store.
5. **Local technicians** — service providers taking on-site repair bookings.
6. **Buyers** — anyone with a broken device who needs a guide, part, expert call, or home service.
7. **Platform admin** — the SobaiShikhi team that reviews, approves, controls, and monetizes everything.

---

## Why would they use my product?

- **Everything in Bangla, in one place** — learn a skill, fix a problem, buy the part, book an expert, hire a technician — without leaving the platform.
- **Trust by design** — verified instructors/experts, admin-reviewed content, ratings, warranties, and escrow-protected payments.
- **Earn from expertise** — instructors, experts, vendors, and technicians all get income streams with transparent commissions and local payouts (bKash/Nagad).
- **Practical, not theoretical** — step-by-step repair guides, hands-on courses, and live expert help solve _real_ problems today.

---

## Top 3 pain points

1. **No trusted Bangla destination** for practical learning + repair help — people waste hours across YouTube, Facebook, and unreliable local shops.
2. **Experts can't monetize knowledge** — no tools to publish courses/guides, build reputation, or get paid reliably.
3. **Broken-device journey is broken** — diagnosing, learning the fix, sourcing the right tool/part, and finding a trustworthy technician are all disconnected and risky.

---

## Simplest version that provides value (MVP)

A **curated Bangla course marketplace + repair knowledge hub** with an admin approval gate:

- Browse / search courses by category; course detail with curriculum, preview, reviews.
- Learner enrollment + dashboard (continue learning, progress).
- Instructor dashboard + course builder → **submit for admin review** → publish.
- Repair Hub: searchable, text-based step-by-step guides (iFixit-style) contributed by experts → admin review → publish.
- **Admin console** that reviews/approves all content and controls the platform.
- Local payments (bKash/Nagad) and verified-instructor badges for trust.

_(The full ecosystem — tools/parts store, expert video consultations, home-service booking, community forum, blog/vlogs, AI repair assistant — layers on after the MVP proves demand.)_

---

## How will this make money?

Multiple commission-based streams (all rates admin-configurable):

**Education**

- Course sales commission (~20%)
- Subscriptions & premium certifications

**Marketplace**

- Tool & spare-parts sales commission (~10%)
- Vendor subscriptions & featured listings

**Services**

- Video consultation commission (~15%)
- Home-service booking commission (~12%)
- Expert subscriptions

**Advertising**

- Sponsored products, featured vendors, sponsored experts

> The combination makes SobaiShikhi a **knowledge economy platform**, not just a course marketplace — many revenue streams from one trusted audience.
