# 07 — Go-to-Market & Growth

How SobaiShikhi acquires its first users, prices its offerings, and grows from a curated launch into Bangladesh's largest knowledge & repair ecosystem. Follows the 3-phase strategy: **curated → instructor expansion → open marketplace.**

---

## A. Positioning
**One line:** *Bangladesh's all-in-one Bangla platform to learn skills, fix problems, and earn from your expertise.*

**Category:** not "another course site" — a **knowledge economy platform** (Udemy + iFixit + JustAnswer + Daraz + Fiverr), localized and trust-first.

**Wedge:** start narrow and deep — **job-ready freelancing/tech + English skills in Bangla** (highest intent, clear ROI), plus a free **Repair Hub** as a top-of-funnel magnet.

---

## B. The cold-start solution (chicken-and-egg)
A marketplace needs supply *and* demand. Sequence it:

1. **Seed supply first (curated).** Hand-invite 30–50 credible instructors + repair experts; help them produce 50–100 premium courses + a starter set of high-search repair guides. Quality over quantity.
   - **Three creation tiers unlock "stuck" experts:** self-produced (keep ~80%) for the capable; **we-produce** (~50–60% platform) for those who can't shoot/edit; **idea buy-out** for evergreen topics we'll own. The managed + buy-out tiers convert otherwise-stuck expertise into catalog — a direct supply lever during the curated phase.
2. **Free Repair Hub as the magnet.** Text-first guides rank in search and pull in problem-driven traffic at near-zero CAC — then cross-sell courses, tools, experts.
3. **Convert audiences instructors already have.** Creators bring their own Facebook/YouTube followings; revenue share motivates them to promote.
4. **Then open demand spend** once conversion + retention are proven.

---

## C. Acquisition channels (by cost/intent)
**Organic / low-CAC (lead here)**
- SEO on repair guides ("[device] [problem] সমাধান") + course landing pages (Bangla search).
- Free classes & guide clips repurposed to **Facebook, YouTube Shorts, TikTok** (where BD learners already are).
- Instructor/expert audiences (their own followers).
- Referral program: learners refer friends → discount credit; instructors refer instructors.

**Paid (scale once economics work)**
- Facebook/Instagram + YouTube ads targeting skill/career intent.
- Google search ads on high-intent repair & course queries.

**Partnerships**
- Universities, polytechnics, freelancer communities, mosque/madrasah networks (religious category), device/repair shops (parts + technician supply).

---

## D. Pricing
**Learners**
- Per-course (৳699–৳2,950 in prototype), heavy launch discounts (up to 60%) for social proof.
- **Subscription** (P5): monthly all-access for casual learners.
- **Free tier:** Repair Hub guides + free preview lessons + free classes — the funnel.

**Marketplace take rates (admin-configurable)**
- Courses ~20% · Store (tools/parts) ~10% · Consultations ~15% · Services ~12%.
- Consultation tiers: ৳199 (15m) · ৳499 (30m) · ৳999 (60m).

**Supply-side incentives**
- Launch: reduced/zero commission for first cohort of instructors.
- Featured placement, payout speed, and verified badges as non-cash incentives.

---

## E. Funnel & conversion loop
`Repair Hub / free class (SEO+social) → account → free preview → first paid course → dashboard habit (streak) → cross-sell (tools, consult, more courses) → certificate → referral`

- **Top:** free guides & clips (awareness, near-zero CAC).
- **Mid:** previews, ratings, verified badges, discounts (trust → first purchase).
- **Bottom:** AI assistant routes a *problem* to learn/buy/consult/hire (monetize intent).
- **Loop:** certificates + streaks + referrals + new categories bring users back.

---

## F. Trust = growth (BD-specific)
Trust is the biggest barrier to online payment & learning in Bangladesh. Make it visible:
- Admin-reviewed stamp, verified instructors/experts/technicians.
- Ratings, success rates, best-answer badges, money-back guarantee.
- Escrow + service warranty on bookings.
- Local payments (bKash/Nagad/Rocket) + Bangla support.

---

## G. Phased GTM (maps to roadmap)
**Phase 1 — Curated launch:** invite-only, 50–100 courses, seed repair guides, founder-led instructor onboarding, social proof via discounts & testimonials.
**Phase 2 — Instructor expansion:** open applications, add ratings/reviews, scale categories, nationwide reach, referral engine on.
**Phase 3 — Open marketplace:** thousands of courses + guides, recommendation engine, paid acquisition at scale, ads & subscriptions, corporate/bulk deals.

---

## H. North-star & growth metrics
- **North star:** *weekly active learners who complete ≥1 lesson/guide* (value delivered, not just signups).
- **Acquisition:** CAC by channel, organic share, repair-guide traffic.
- **Activation:** signup→first lesson, signup→first purchase.
- **Engagement/retention:** streaks, WAU/MAU, course completion %.
- **Marketplace:** GMV, AOV, take rate, instructor/vendor activation, supply growth.
- **Loop:** referral coefficient, AI-assistant → order conversion.

---

## I. First-90-days launch checklist
1. Lock 30–50 founding instructors/experts; produce 50–100 courses + 20–30 repair guides.
2. Ship Phase-1 MVP; enable bKash/Nagad + review gate.
3. Publish Repair Hub guides for top-searched BD problems (SEO).
4. Launch referral + 60%-off founding-learner offer.
5. Run founder-led webinars/lives in target categories; repurpose to social.
6. Instrument funnel + review-queue turnaround; iterate weekly on the biggest drop-off.
